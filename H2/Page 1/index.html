Origins of Anime

Anime originated in Japan on the date 1917, pretty much a century ago. The first anime created was by Oten Shimokawa, It was made with chalk and only lasted around 5 minutes or so. Most of the old animations created back then were destroyed by the Great Kanto Earthquake in 1923, by the world war two bombings or either decayed because of old age.

Why is anime possibly so popular?

Anime has rather nice aspects like storytelling, beautiful animation and art-style, Great fight/action scenes, charachters have a unique personality, easy to access because of the internet, wide range of genres, topics and subjects.
